Security Film

Security film is one of the most effective ways to strengthen vulnerable glass. By bonding directly to the window, our film holds shattered glass together, making it significantly harder for intruders to gain entry. This added layer of protection gives businesses and homeowners peace of mind, knowing their property and the people inside are safer against break-ins, vandalism, and unexpected riots.

Protection Against Riots & Intruders

Worried about riots, looting, or smash-and-grab break-ins? Our security film reinforces your existing glass, making it far more difficult for intruders to break through. With fast and easy installation, your windows become a powerful barrier, keeping your property secure 24/7 and giving you peace of mind.

Weather Resistance

Severe weather can cause devastating damage when glass shatters. Our film is engineered to hold glass together during hurricanes, tornados, monsoons, blizzards, or high winds. By keeping windows intact, you reduce the risk of further property damage and ensure greater safety inside.

UV Protection & Energy Efficiency

Riot Defense security film does more than strengthen glass—it helps protect your interiors and lower energy costs. By blocking harmful UV rays, our film prevents fading of furniture, carpets, and drapes while also reducing heat buildup. Pair it with our tint or decorative films for added privacy, comfort, and style.
